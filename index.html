PFAS Testing in Food, Beverages & Food Packaging

End-to-end LC-MS/MS solution for quantitation of per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances in complex food matrices. Covers dietary exposure monitoring per EFSA guidance, including ultra-short-chain PFAS below existing reference points.

Click any step to see recommended products

QuEChERS PFAS Extraction Kit & ISOLUTE ENV+ SPE
Buffered QuEChERS extraction optimised for polar PFAS in produce and processed food. ISOLUTE ENV+ polystyrene sorbent gives >80% recovery across C4–C14 chain lengths.
View products for this step
Bond Elut PFAS Cartridges (WAX sorbent)
Mixed-mode WAX sorbent removes matrix interferences from food extracts while retaining all regulated PFAS. Low blank background critical for ultra-trace work.
View products for this step
1290 Infinity II HPLC + ZORBAX Eclipse Plus C18
1200 bar system with delay column configuration eliminates PFAS background bleed from PEEK tubing. ZORBAX Eclipse C18 (2.1×100 mm, 1.8 µm) retains ultra-short-chain species cleanly.
View products for this step
Agilent 6470B Triple Quadrupole LC/MS
500 MRM transitions with dynamic scheduling. Jet Stream ESI handles high-matrix food extracts without signal suppression. Achieves 0.01 µg/kg LOQ in fresh produce.
View products for this step
MassHunter Quant Navigator + PFAS Reporting Template
Automated batch processing with regulatory limit flagging. Pre-built PFAS template maps all 40 compounds to EFSA dietary exposure thresholds. 21 CFR Part 11 compliant audit trail.
