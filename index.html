PFAS Testing in Food, Beverages & Food Packaging

End-to-end LC-MS/MS solution for quantitation of per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances in complex food matrices. Covers dietary exposure monitoring per EFSA guidance, including ultra-short-chain PFAS below existing reference points.

40+
PFAS compounds quantified
0.01 µg/kg
LOQ in fresh produce
validated
Against EFSA 2020 guidance & EC 2023/915

End-to-end solution workflow

Each step connects to catalog PDPs for commerce and eRFQ. Click a step to see recommended products.

Step 1
Sample extraction
QuEChERS PFAS extraction kit · ISOLUTE ENV+
Step 2
SPE cleanup
Bond Elut PFAS cartridges · WAX sorbent
Step 3
LC separation
1290 Infinity II HPLC · ZORBAX Eclipse Plus C18
Step 4
MS/MS detection
6470B Triple Quad LC/MS · 500 MRM transitions
Step 5
Data analysis
MassHunter Quant Navigator · PFAS reporting template
Instruments for this application

Agilent 6470B Triple Quadrupole LC/MS

Primary detection platform. 500 MRM transitions, dynamic MRM scheduling, Jet Stream ESI — optimised for trace PFAS in high-matrix food extracts.

Validated for this application

Agilent 1290 Infinity II LC System

High-pressure LC for complex food matrices. Delay column configuration prevents PFAS background from LC system tubing.

Validated for this application

ZORBAX Eclipse Plus C18 Column

2.1 × 100 mm, 1.8 µm. Optimised retention for short-chain PFAS. Inert, PFAS-free frit and end-fittings.

Validated for this application
